The DevOps course, known as COMP3104 is offered in the fifth semester of the T177 Program.

It introduces key concepts and practices of DevOps, and how it enhances the productivity in software development.

The course contains multiple hands-on labs, as well as weekly quizzes to test the knowledge of students.

Students explore version control systems and how to manage low-risk software releases effectively. 

The course also covers cloud computing, emphasizing Infrastructure as Code and its benefits.

By the end of the course, students will have practical skills and knowledge to contribute to DevOps practices in any organization.
