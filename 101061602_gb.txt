George Brown College is a public, fully accredited college of applied arts and technology located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It offers a variety of programs in different disciplines including technology, business, and health sciences.

History:
Established in 1967, George Brown College was named after George Brown, a 19th-century politician and newspaper publisher who was one of the Fathers of Confederation in Canada.
The college has grown to offer programs in various disciplines such as technology, business, health sciences, community services, and the arts.
